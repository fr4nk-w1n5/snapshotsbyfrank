Hi! My name is Frank Mwangi and I am a photographer based in Seattle. I mainly do portrait work, but this also includes corporate pictures, professional headshots, family group pictures, as well as events such as weddings.

I got into photography after taking an introductory course at my University. It has since then become a passion of mine and I really take pride in capturing special moments for people.

I use a Sony Alpha A7R III as my main camera. I have different lenses to accomodate different client needs.
